Who I am

I’m Yentl Tijssens (also known as Cobe) and I’m a musician.
I study classical music in Brussels and I play piano and cello. Outside school I also make electronic music. Everything from house music to soundtrack for movies. For making music I use Ableton Live on my Macbook together with a Korg keyboard, Novation Launchpad, Krk Rokit’s and a cup of coffee.
